Easiest Roasted Turkey

(within 2 hours)

INGREDIENTS

  1. One 12- to 18-pound turkey, thawed. You can keep the giblets and nect if you like.
  2. Two medium onions, peeled and quartered.
  3. Two stalks celery, cleaned and roughly chopped.
  4. Three tablespoons kosher salt.
  5. One and half tablespoons ground black pepper.
  6. Six tablespoons unsalted butter, softened and cut into small pats.
  7. Optional: thyme, sage, rosemary...any herbs you like.

PREPARATION

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees. Place the turkey on a cutting board with its breast side down, and using a very sharp knife to cut out the birds's backbone.
  2. Turn the turkey over and remove the legs and thighs. Press down on the turkey breasts to flatten the carcass.
  3. Place the turkey parts in one large roasting pan. Tuck between them onion, celery pieces, and herbs if you use them.
  4. Shower the bird generously with salt and pepper. Dot turkey pieces with pasts of butter.
  5. Place pan in a 450 degree oven for 30 minutes, then reduce the heat to 325 degress and baste with accumulated juices.
  6. Put the pan back to the 325 degree oven for another 30 to 45 minutes. Check the temperature of the breast and the thigh.
  7. Remove the bird from the oven when the temperature reached 165 degree. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  8. Time to eat!
